# Ticket-to-flight
Ticket to flight is an economic strategy heavily inspired by board games "Age of steam" and "Ticket to ride". Players play as an aviation companies in the age of the fast growth of air transportation market. There was an intense competition back then and we've tried to recreate it in our game: issue shares, participate in auctions, fight for the passengers, buy new planes, rent new airlines, always be on the leading edge of technological advancments and generate tons of money! But don't forget that at any moment other player can interrupt your plans or a devastating world event may heppen knoking you from the top of the market down to its very bottom.

More detailed information about the game, including the rules, can be found in the "Game rules" file. 
The libraries and frameworks used in this project are listed in the "technologies" file.
