# Ticket-to-flight
Ticket to flight is an economic strategy heavily inspired by board games "Age of steam" and "Ticket to ride". Players play as aviation companies in an age of the fast growth of air transportation market. There was an intense competition back then and we've tried to recreate it in our game: issue shares, participate in auctions, fight for the passengers, buy new planes, rent new air routs, always be on the leading edge of technological advancments and generate tons of money! But don't forget that at any moment other player can interrupt your plans, or a devastating world event may happen knocking you from the top of the market down to its very bottom.

More detailed information about the game, including the rules, can be found in the "Game rules" file. 
The libraries and frameworks used in this project are listed in the "technologies" file.
